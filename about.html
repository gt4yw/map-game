About BattleGuessr

Guess the year of famous battles, just based on the map.

BattleGuessr is enabled by your contributions. If you have any maps that you would like to see in the game, please submit them to this form.

Game Modes

  • Battle Mode: Maps of historical wars, battles, and troop movements
  • Daily Map Mode: Daily challenge
  • Weekly MapMode (Hard): More challenging historical maps
