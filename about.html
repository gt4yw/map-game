About MapGuessr

MapGuessr is a game that challenges your knowledge of historical maps. Test your ability to identify the time period of various historical maps across different difficulty levels.

MapGuessr is supported by your contributions. If you have any maps that you would like to see in the game, please submit them to this form.

Game Modes

  • Daily Mode: Daily challenge
  • Weekly Mode (Hard): Challenging historical maps for experts
  • Battle Mode: Maps of historical wars, battles, and troop movements
